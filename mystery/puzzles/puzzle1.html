PUZZLE #1

Puzzle Goal: Library Research

Hints:

Water and the mandrake are emblems of this first born Tribe of Judah.

Find it's name in the documents of yore.

The Guardian Scribe that protects and shares knowledge will verify your research.

Remember to be in and out of the room in about 8 minutes.

After you have completed your research, you may open the gift box to reveal your mystery findings.


After You Have Solved the Puzzle - Before Leaving the Room -

After you have investigated the contents of the gift box and recorded your findings in your notebook, please:

  • Place the items (e.g. receipt/order form, gemstones, etc.) back in the box
  • Put the documents back as you found them./li>
  • Please leave the room tidy, and as you found it
  • Do not remove clues, decorations, evidence, etc.! Make notes of it or take pictures of it instead.
