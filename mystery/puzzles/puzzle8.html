PUZZLE #8

Puzzle Goal: Successfully Play At Least One Round of 1940s Pictionary

Instructions:

  • Elect a person to do the drawing (the rest in the group guesses)
  • The person to do the drawing selects a task (e.g. a film title) from the draw-string bag (don't show it to the others)
  • Use the whiteboard to draw (without writing words) clues to get the others to make a correct guess (e.g. the correct film title)
  • Please pay attention to time and begin exiting the room within 8 minutes of entering
  • After successfully playing one or more rounds, you have earned the rights to look the gift box for evidence!
  • Don't forget to look around the room for other evidence

After You Have Solved the Puzzle - Before Leaving the Room -

After you have investigated the contents of the gift box and recorded your findings in your notebook, please:

  • Place any drawn items (e.g. film titles) back into the draw-string bag
  • Whipe the whiteboard clean
  • Please leave the room tidy, and as you found it - do not remove clues, decorations, evidence, etc.! Make notes of it or take pictures of it instead.
