PUZZLE #12

Puzzle Goal: Open The Safe

Instructions:

  • Find the key hidden in this room - then use it to open the safe.

Clue:

  • The name for the Nauvoo Temple's first stone teaches us about finding the Savior's role in our lives. That name is a clue for finding the key.

After You Have Solved the Puzzle - Before Leaving the Room -

After opening the safe, please:

  • Replace items from the safe.
  • Hide the key back where you found it.
  • Please leave the room tidy, and as you found it
  • Do not remove clues, decorations, evidence, etc.! Make notes of it or take pictures of it instead.
