PUZZLE #6

Puzzle Goal: Open The Cryptix

Instructions:

  • Arrange the dials on the Cryptix such that it can be opened.
  • There are 7 dials, each having 8 possible settings. That means there are 2,097,152 possible combinations.
  • Your hands are probably not fast enough to try all possibilities!
  • Notice that there are a few other containers here - could they be useful? Maybe they are easier to open.
  • Please pay attention to time and begin exiting the room within 8 minutes of entering
  • Once you have opened the cryptix, you have also earned the right to open the gift box.
  • Don't forget to look around the room for other evidence

After You Have Solved the Puzzle - Before Leaving the Room -

After you have investigated the contents of the containers and cryptix and gift box and recorded your findings in your notebook, please:

  • Place the scroll back into the cryptix, close it and scramble the dials
  • Place the code hints back into the containers, and close them (reverse-solve the puzzles - which is much easier if you watch the path).
  • Please leave the room tidy, and as you found it
  • Do not remove clues, decorations, evidence, etc.! Make notes of it or take pictures of it instead.
