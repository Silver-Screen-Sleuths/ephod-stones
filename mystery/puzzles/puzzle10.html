PUZZLE #10

Puzzle Goal: Film Charades

Instructions:

  • Choose one person to act out a 1940s film title.
  • This person will choose a film from the drawstring bag.
  • The other team members will then attempt to guess the film title
  • Once the film title has been guessed correctly, open the Evidence Envelope which contains your next clue.

After You Have Solved the Puzzle - Before Leaving the Room -

After you have finnished, return everthing to where you found it, please:

  • Place film title back into the drawstring bag.
  • Return the clue to the Evidence Envelope.
  • Please leave the room tidy, and as you found it
  • Do not remove clues, decorations, evidence, etc.! Make notes of it or take pictures of it instead.
