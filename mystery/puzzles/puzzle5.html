PUZZLE #5

Puzzle Goal: Test Your Knowledge of 1940s Pop Culture and Politics

Instructions:

  • Work in teams of two or more to answer the sixteen questions.

  • The stone is hidden somewhere in the room along with clues to gain more information.

After You Have Solved the Puzzle - Before Leaving the Room -

Return everything back to where you found it:

  • Please leave the room tidy, and as you found it
  • Do not remove clues, decorations, evidence, etc.! Make notes of it or take pictures of it instead.
